Help Pro

*App Designer*

An organizing app that helps you time manage and stay focused.

Sketches

Calender

The calendar pages provide different ways to see what you have scheduled.Schedule/1 day 3 days Week

When making a new event using the "+" on the right-hand side, you can set if you want the event to be a deep focus or not. Deep focus allow you to automatically turn your phone/notifacations off so you can stay focused. You also pick which apps you would need to stay focused on that task.

Study Room

The study room is where you can have study groups with your peers. On this page, I wanted an AI that could help the person studying with tips if they asked questions like:

- What are the different ways I can structure my essay?

- Can you show me a video of how to do limits?

Focused Timer

The Focused timer is where you have a time that you set to stay focused. I'm hoping to put a feature on this page so the longer you study the more money you give to the charity of your choice.
